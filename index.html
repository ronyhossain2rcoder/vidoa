Privacy Policy

Package: com.bright.player.vidoa

Overview

Vidoa is a video player app designed to let you enjoy your local and online video content. The app does not collect or store any personal user data directly. However, it may integrate third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Analytics. These services may automatically collect some non-personal information to provide ads, analyze app usage, and improve performance.

Third-Party Services Used

  • Google AdMob – Used to display ads. May use Advertising ID and cookies to personalize ads and measure performance.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – Used for crash reporting and stability monitoring.
  • Firebase Analytics – Used to understand app usage and improve user experience.

Required Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Access video files stored on your device.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – Access video content in your media library.
  • INTERNET – Stream online videos and show ads.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Check network connectivity for streaming.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK – Enable continuous video playback in the foreground.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – Support background services.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Notify you about playback, updates, or other app events.
